# ADMIT — Interview Question Bank
### Who to talk to, and every question to ask, for SACLI Admissions/Enrollment content collection

---

## How to use this

Each section below = one office/person + every question relevant to their area. You don't need to ask every single question verbatim — treat this as a checklist so nothing falls through the cracks, and let the conversation flow naturally. Bring a recorder or take notes directly into the "Content Entries" tab of the KB template as you go; each answer you get is close to a finished KB row.

**Suggested order:** Registrar's Office first (they touch almost every category), then Accounting, then CCO, then each department head, then Scholarship/Guidance last (smaller, more specific scope).

---

## 1. Registrar's Office
*(Covers: admission requirements, enrollment process, records, general procedural questions — the backbone of the knowledge base)*

**Admission Requirements**
1. What documents are required for a new Grade 1/incoming Kindergarten student?
2. What documents are required for a new SHS student?
3. What documents are required for a new HED (college) student/freshman?
4. What documents are required for a TESDA applicant?
5. Is there an entrance exam? For which levels/programs? What does it cover?
6. Is there an interview as part of admission? Who conducts it?
7. What is the minimum age requirement per level?
8. Are there GWA/academic requirements for admission to specific programs?
9. What happens if an applicant is missing a document — can they enroll conditionally?
10. Is there a limit on the number of slots per program/section? What happens when a program is full?
11. What is the admission requirement for international or foreign transferee students, if any?
12. What is the requirement for ALS (Alternative Learning System) completers applying to SHS/HED?

**Enrollment Process — New Students**
13. What is the exact step-by-step enrollment process for a brand-new student, from inquiry to being officially enrolled?
14. Where physically does each step happen (which office, which window/room)?
15. Can enrollment be done online or partially online? What platform?
16. What is the enrollment schedule/timeline for the upcoming school year (opening date, deadline, late enrollment cutoff)?
17. Is there a fee for late enrollment?

**Enrollment Process — Transferees**
18. What additional documents does a transferee need (Form 137/138, TOR, honorable dismissal, etc.)?
19. Is there a credit evaluation process for transferees? Who handles it and how long does it take?
20. Can a transferee enroll mid-year/mid-semester?
21. Are there subjects a transferee might need to take as "bridging" or removal classes?

**Enrollment Process — Returnees / Continuing Students**
22. What is the process for a returning student (LOA/stopped out) to re-enroll?
23. What is the process for a continuing student re-enrolling each term (subject enlistment, clearance requirements)?
24. Is a clearance from the previous term required before re-enrolling (library, accounting, discipline)?

**Cross-cutting / Records**
25. What are the Registrar's Office hours and contact details (phone, email, office location)?
26. What are the most common questions or complaints your office receives from applicants/parents?
27. Are there common misunderstandings or misinformation you keep having to correct?
28. Is there an existing FAQ document, enrollment guide, or flyer we can use as a source?
29. How does someone request a Certificate of Enrollment, TOR, or other document, and how long does it take?
30. What is the process and requirement for shifting programs/strands/courses?

## 2. Accounting / Cashier / Finance Office
*(Covers: tuition, fees, payment schemes)*

31. What is the tuition fee range per level (IBED, SHS, HED, TESDA)? Is it per year, per semester, or per grading period?
32. What other fees are collected aside from tuition (miscellaneous, laboratory, ID, insurance, etc.)?
33. What payment schemes are offered (full payment, installment, per-grading-period)?
34. What are the accepted modes of payment (cash, bank deposit, online banking, GCash, credit card, etc.)?
35. Are there discounts for early payment, full payment, or for siblings enrolled together?
36. What happens if a student misses an installment deadline — is there a penalty, and can enrollment be affected?
37. Is there a specific down payment amount required to enroll?
38. What is the refund policy if a student withdraws after paying?
39. Are there different fee structures for new vs. continuing students?
40. What are your office hours and how can students reach you with billing questions?
41. What is the process to request an Official Receipt or statement of account?
42. Are there fees specific to certain HED programs (e.g., lab fees for Nursing, uniform fees) worth noting separately?

## 3. Scholarship / Financial Assistance Office (or whichever office manages this — may be Registrar or a separate unit)

43. What scholarship programs does SACLI currently offer (academic, athletic, sibling discount, employee dependent, government-funded like ESC/TES/TDP, etc.)?
44. What are the eligibility requirements for each scholarship?
45. What is the application process and what documents are needed?
46. What is the application deadline for each scholarship, and when are results released?
47. Is the scholarship renewable each year/term? What GWA or condition must be maintained to keep it?
48. How many slots are available per scholarship type?
49. Can a student apply for more than one scholarship or financial assistance program at once?
50. What happens if a scholar fails to meet the retention requirement mid-year?
51. Who should a student contact with scholarship questions (name/office/contact info)?

## 4. Corporate Communications Office (CCO)
*(Covers: general institutional info, first-touch inquiries, brand-level messaging)*

52. What are SACLI's official mission, vision, and core values (verbatim, as officially published)?
53. What academic levels and departments does SACLI currently offer (confirm IBED, SHS, HED, TESDA are all still accurate and complete)?
54. What are the general contact details for the institution as a whole (main line, official email, official social media pages, office address)?
55. What are the most frequently asked questions your office receives via social media, walk-ins, or phone?
56. Are there common pieces of misinformation circulating online (Facebook groups, forums) that the institution wants to correct?
57. Are there any current promotions, announcements, or events prospective students should know about (open house, campus tour schedule)?
58. What is SACLI's brand tone/voice when communicating with prospective students and parents — formal, warm, conversational?
59. Is there an official school calendar or academic year schedule (start of classes, holidays, exam periods) we should reference?
60. Are there any topics or questions your office prefers a chatbot NOT answer directly, and instead redirect to a human (e.g., complaints, legal, sensitive matters)?

## 5. IBED Department Head (Kindergarten–Grade 10)

61. What grade levels and sections are currently offered under IBED?
62. Are there any specialized programs or tracks within IBED (STEM enrichment, SPED, etc.)?
63. What subjects/curriculum framework does IBED follow (DepEd K-12 standard, or any add-ons like a signature curriculum)?
64. Are there specific admission requirements unique to IBED beyond the general Registrar requirements (e.g., age cutoff per grade, readiness assessment for Kindergarten)?
65. What extracurricular programs, clubs, or facilities are notable for IBED (clinic, library, sports)?
66. Who should a parent contact with IBED-specific academic questions?

## 6. Senior High School (SHS) Coordinator/Head

67. What strands/tracks does SACLI SHS currently offer (STEM, ABM, HUMSS, GAS, TVL, etc.)?
68. What are the subject offerings or specializations under each strand?
69. Is there a specific admission requirement or exam per strand?
70. Are there government voucher programs (ESC, SHS Voucher Program) applicable, and how does a student avail of them?
71. What career or college pathways does each strand typically lead to (useful for chatbot to explain "why choose this strand")?
72. What is the enrollment process specific to incoming Grade 11 students (coming from Grade 10, whether from SACLI or another school)?
73. Who should students/parents contact with SHS-specific questions?

## 7. HED (College) Deans/Program Heads — repeat per program/department
*(Interview each dean/head separately if programs vary significantly, e.g., Business, IT, Education, Nursing, etc.)*

74. What degree programs are currently offered under this department?
75. What is the curriculum overview or notable subjects for each program?
76. Are there specific admission requirements for this program (entrance exam, minimum grade, interview, portfolio, etc.)?
77. Are there licensure/board exam considerations, and what is the program's track record (if okay to share)?
78. Is there a required OJT/internship, and when does it typically happen in the curriculum?
79. What facilities, laboratories, or partnerships are unique to this program (useful selling points for prospective students)?
80. Are there year-level or block-sectioning policies students should know about?
81. Who should a prospective student contact with questions specific to this program?

## 8. TESDA Program Coordinator

82. What TESDA courses/qualifications does SACLI currently offer (e.g., NC II courses)?
83. What are the admission requirements for TESDA programs (age, educational attainment, documents)?
84. What is the schedule format (full-time, part-time, modular)?
85. What is the duration of each course/program?
86. What is the assessment and certification process at the end of the course (NC II assessment, fees involved)?
87. What are the fees for TESDA programs, and do they follow the same payment schemes as HED/SHS or differ?
88. Are there partnerships or job-placement assistance after certification?
89. Who should applicants contact with TESDA-specific questions?

## 9. Guidance Office (if separate from Registrar)

90. Is a guidance interview or psychological exam part of the admission process? For which levels?
91. What is the process and purpose of this interview/exam?
92. Does the Guidance Office handle any part of scholarship screening or student welfare concerns relevant to enrollment?
93. Who should a parent contact for guidance-related concerns?

## 10. IT/MIS Office or existing Helpdesk/Social Media Team (if applicable)

95. Is there an existing FAQ page on the school website we can pull from and verify?
96. Are there any online enrollment or student portal systems currently in use that ADMIT should be aware of or reference?

## General / Ask Everyone

97. What is the single most common question your office gets asked, and how do you usually answer it?
98. What's a question you get asked that you WISH people already knew the answer to?
99. Is there anything applicants/parents frequently misunderstand about your office's process?
100. What's the best way for someone to reach your office directly if the chatbot can't fully answer their question (phone, email, office hours, location)?

## If time is limited — the shortlist to prioritize first

If Ortega can only get a handful of interviews done before development needs to start, this subset alone is enough to seed a usable first version of ADMIT:

- Registrar: Q1–3, 13, 16, 25 (basic requirements, enrollment steps, office contact)
- Accounting: Q31, 33, 34, 38 (tuition, payment schemes, modes, refund)
- CCO: Q52, 54, 59 (mission/vision, general contact info, academic calendar)
- One rep per department (IBED/SHS/HED/TESDA): just Q61/67/74/82 (what's offered) + admission requirement

Everything else can be filled in during later iterations — this matches the thesis's own Agile/iterative framing (Ch. III), where the knowledge base is expected to expand across cycles rather than be complete on day one.
